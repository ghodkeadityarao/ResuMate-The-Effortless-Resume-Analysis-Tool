  • The use of passive voice in a resume is generally discouraged, as it can make your achievements and contributions sound less impressive and may cause your resume to come across as weak or unclear.
  • Passive voice refers to sentences in which the subject of the sentence is receiving the action, rather than performing it.

  • Here's an example of how to use passive voices in a resume:
  • Before: The team completed the project
  • After: The project was completed by the team
